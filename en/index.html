Our Company

Our daily focus is to achieve perfection in business both personally and as a team.
What is in our name is a criterion that has become a commitment from all aspects and elements of company activities. The company name Aglonera Sampada Nusantara means 'Plants that bring perfection or prosperity "new", "pure", "natural", "fresh"' and thus our responsibility to customers is a reflection of our company name.
PT. Aglonera Sampada Nusantara (ASN) is a multi-agricultural commodity company. We provide integrated solutions to our companies/partners. Our company was born from the world of collection and trading and now the company is managed by professional staff.

Our approach is to provide solutions, enrich society, create added value and profitable growth. We believe in hard work, employee engagement and sustainable innovation.

Warehouse & Facilities

The warehouse of PT. Aglonera Sampada Nusantara & its supporting facilities is a supporting system from fields and plantations around Nusantara to ensure our high quality and quality standards. Our warehouse is located in West Java and Jakarta areas.

OUR STRATEGICALLY LOCATED WAREHOUSE DETERMINES OUR MAXIMUM PERFORMANCE

In implementing our company strategy, we build our facilities in harvest center areas to achieve the best effectiveness in harvest collection and logistics convenience. We collect, store, and process commodities to ensure maximum supply for our customers.

Our Featured Products

We provide various high-quality agricultural commodities from Nusantara.

Coconut

Fresh coconuts from selected trees, harvested using traditional techniques that preserve natural quality. Perfect for food, oil, and cosmetic industries.

Premium quality with fresh water content and thick flesh. Harvested directly from the best coconut plantations in Indonesia.

Ginger

Premium red and white ginger with high gingerol content. Harvested from organic fields, ideal for pharmaceutical, food, and health beverage industries.

Cultivated organically without chemical pesticides. High gingerol content provides optimal health benefits.

Nutmeg

Export-quality dried nutmeg with distinctive aroma and flavor. Processed to international standards for spice and pharmaceutical industries.

Harvested from mature nutmeg trees producing seeds with high essential oil content. Natural drying process preserves quality.

Coffee

Arabica and Robusta coffee from Indonesian highlands. Hand-picked and processed using traditional techniques to produce unique and premium quality taste.

Grown at altitudes of 1200-1800 masl with ideal climate. Natural fermentation process produces distinctive flavor characteristics.
